A snowey place full of tourists and helpul and cheerful local people always ready to help you. Legned has it that if you are going on a treck to the top of the base camp or to climb the mountain A dog will guide you the paths as long as you give it food to eat.

There are things to do for everyone here. If you like to go on a long trecks with the breath taking views of the Anapurna City you can do that and if you can't do longs walks there are varius viewpoints in closer waking distance where you can enjoy the view with the nice hot tea.
